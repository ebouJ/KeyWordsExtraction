Edward "Eddie" Gleeson (born 1920) is an Irish retired hurler who played as a left corner-forward for the Tipperary senior team.
Gleeson made his first appearance for the team during the 1945 championship and was a regular member of the starting fifteen for just two full seasons. During that time he won one All-Ireland medal and one Munster medal.
At club level Gleeson played with Thurles Sarsfields.
